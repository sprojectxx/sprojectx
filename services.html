Ask Us Anything
Is the free website really free?
Yes, 100% free. We design and deliver a custom static website at no charge. The only thing you need to provide is your brand details and content.
What's the catch with the free plan?
There's no catch. The free website will be hosted on a Vercel subdomain (e.g. yourcafe.vercel.app). If you want your own custom domain and advanced features, that's the premium upgrade.
How long does it take to build?
We deliver your website in 3–5 business days from receiving your complete brand details, logo, and any content you want included.
Can I request revisions?
Yes. We offer up to 2 rounds of revisions on the free plan and unlimited revisions on the premium plan. Your satisfaction is our priority.
Do I need to provide content?
Ideally yes — your logo, images, menu, and a short description. But if you don't have these, we can work with placeholder content and update later.

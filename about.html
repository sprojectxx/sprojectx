The Team
Behind the Aesthetic

A passionate group of designers and developers dedicated to crafting modern, aesthetic digital experiences for cafes and restaurants worldwide.

Our Story

Born From a Love of
Beautiful Spaces

Sprojectxx was founded with a simple observation — most cafes and restaurants have incredible physical ambiance, but their digital presence doesn't do them justice.

We saw the gap: cookie-cutter templates, generic layouts, and expensive agency quotes that left small business owners underserved. So we decided to change that.

Today, Sprojectxx is the go-to design partner for hospitality businesses who want a digital presence that truly reflects their brand — starting completely free.

Our Mission

Helping You Own
Your Digital Space

To help cafes and restaurants establish a strong digital presence through visually stunning and user-friendly websites — without the financial barrier of entry.

We believe every great cafe deserves a great website, regardless of budget. That's why we always start free.

Our Vision

The Go-To Studio for
Hospitality Design

To become the most trusted and recognized design team for aesthetic and minimalistic website solutions in the food and hospitality industry — globally.

Every project we take on is a step toward that vision: one beautiful cafe website at a time.

Our Philosophy

How We Think About Design

01
Aesthetic-First Approach
Every element is chosen with visual intent. Beauty isn't an afterthought — it's the foundation. We believe your website should be as beautiful as your interior.
02
Minimalistic Layouts
We remove the noise so your brand can breathe. Clean whitespace, intentional typography, and a restrained color palette create websites that feel calm and premium.
03
Mobile-First Design
Over 70% of cafe discovery happens on mobile. We design every pixel with the phone screen in mind first, ensuring a flawless experience on every device.
04
Fast-Loading Websites
A slow website kills conversions. We optimize every image, script, and line of code so your site loads in under 2 seconds — keeping visitors engaged.
05
Conversion Focused
Beautiful is great, but functional is essential. Every layout decision is made to guide visitors toward action — visiting, ordering, or calling your cafe.
06
Brand Authenticity
No templates. Every site starts with a conversation about your unique brand story, ensuring the final design feels like yours — not anyone else's.
